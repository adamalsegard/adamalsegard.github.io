About

I'm currently working on my Master Thesis project, which is to develop rendering algorithms for the OpenSpace project at the Visualization Center C in Norrköping. After that I'm looking forward to start working at some interesting company at the end of the summer, maybe you can help me with an idea on where that may be!?


Previously I've been a Master of Science student in Media Technology/Software Engineering at Linköping University (Campus Norrköping), Sweden. As part of my education I spent my forth year at the National University of Singapore as an exchange student. It was a very rewarding experience, both knowledge-wise and for personal growth.


I'm a huge film nerd, both watching and making them. The latter something I got to try while studying film-making in Stockholm for a year before starting University. The main reason I chose the MT program was actually film and the possibility to work with animated movies after graduation. So it should come as no surprise that computer graphics is another big passion of mine!


However, I'd never programmed before starting MT so it took some time to get comfortable but now I love it, especially to get visual results from the code you write. That's why another passion of mine is to write code that's fast (why not parallel), well structured and gives a visual result! I'm very interested in the user experience in the digital world!


When I'm not coding I like to distribute my time between different projects and organizations as well as playing basketball or relaxing with a good book. The purpose of this website is to present both my coding projects but also my other commitments, as well as a playground for me to learn more. Hope you enjoy it!
