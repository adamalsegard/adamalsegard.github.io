About

I'm a software engineer who currently works with medical visualization at Sectra Imaging IT in Linköping, Sweden. My team is responsible for how radiology images are visualized (in 2D, 3D and 4D) within Sectra's PACS software, IDS7. For more information about my professional work, please see my LinkedIn page.

I hold a Master of Science (M.Sc.) degree from Linköping University (LiU) in Media Technology/Computer Scinece and Engineering. It's a software engneering program with extra focus on graphic rendering techniques, AI, UX, AR/VR and visualizations in addition to the standard engineering courses. As part of my education I spent my forth year at the National University of Singapore (NUS) as an exchange student. It was a very rewarding experience, both knowledge-wise and for personal growth.

During my final semester (spring 2018) I worked on my master thesis project, which was about large-scale astrophysical visualization. The project was to incorporate the Gaia DR2 dataset of 1,7 billion stars into the open-source software OpenSpace and then be able to interact with the data in real-time. The project was very successful and I got to showcase my implementation for more than 100 astronomers at the Gaia Sprint 2018 in NYC at the Hayden Planetarium. See my projects for more information.

Besides programming I got a huge interest in film, both wathing them and making them. The latter something I got to try while studying film-making in Stockholm for a year before starting University. The main reason I chose the MT program was actually film and the possibility to work with special effects or animated movies after graduation. So it should come as no surprise that computer graphics is a big passion of mine!

Even though I'd never written a single line of code before starting studying at the University I now love it, especially the problem solving part of it, as well as to get a visual result of the written code. That's why another passion of mine is to write code that's fast (why not parallel), well structured and (hopefully) gives a visual result! I'm also interested in improving the user experience in the digital world!

When I'm not coding or watching movies I like to distribute my time between different projects and organizations as well as traveling, hiking, playing basketball or relaxing with a good book. The purpose of this website is to present both my coding projects and my other commitments, as well as being a playground for me to learn more. Hope you enjoy it!
