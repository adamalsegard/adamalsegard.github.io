GR0WTH

"In a future where all vegetation has died, a company succeeds in producing a synthetic substitute for food. What could have been the dawn of a new era of science turns out to only be a different sort of extinction..."

A sci-fi short film made by Fifth Floor Films, which consists of Adam Alsegård, Oscar Nord, Adrian Palmquist and Benjamin Wiberg. We are a group of students at the Master of Science in Media Technology programme at Linköping University.

Shot with Canon Mark III in and around Norrköping. The film was made as a project at Linköping University in autumn 2013.
